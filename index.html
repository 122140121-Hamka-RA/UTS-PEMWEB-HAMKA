About Saber

Role: Servant

Gender: Female

Saber is the noble and stoic embodiment of Artoria Pendragon—a female King Arthur who wields the legendary sword Excalibur. She has served under various masters, including Emiya Kiritsugu (Fate/Zero), Emiya Shirou (Fate/Stay Night, Unlimited Blade Works, Fate/Hollow Ataraxia), Tohsaka Rin (Unlimited Blade Works), and Matou Sakura (Heaven's Feel). Clad in shining armor with golden hair flowing like a lion’s mane, she radiates grace, power, and an unwavering sense of duty. Though fierce in battle and a master of swordsmanship, Saber hides a heart burdened with guilt and longing, questioning the sacrifices she made for her ideals. Her journey is a blend of strength and vulnerability, making her an unforgettable figure who symbolizes hope, loyalty, and the unyielding spirit of a true king.
